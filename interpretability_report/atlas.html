Labelling Method

How Part-Vote Labels Are Produced

1

Scan Owner-Class Images

For each class-owned prototype, compute the prototype's maximum activation and activation center on images from its owner class.

2

Keep Strong Activations

Use a prototype-specific activation percentile, e.g. top 25%, so weak incidental activations do not dominate the label.

3

Split Vote By Distance

For each retained image, visible landmarks receive Gaussian proximity weights from the activation center.

4

Aggregate Weighted Votes

Each part receives activation × normalized Gaussian weight; the largest total vote becomes the prototype label.
